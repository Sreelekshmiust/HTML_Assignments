Benefits of Node.js

1.Node.js allows to use javascript for both server-side and client-side programming.
2.It allows efficient handling of concurrent connections for real time application.
3.Handles large numbers of simultaneous connections with minimal consumption.
4.Vast collection of open-source libraries and frameworks available through npm.
5.Built on the speedy V8 JavaScript engine, offering high performance.
6.Runs on various operating systems without modification.
7.Well-suited for building and managing microservices-based architectures.
